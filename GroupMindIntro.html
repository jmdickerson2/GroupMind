Here's how GroupMind works!

Step 1: Come up with your course and think of a lesson for each day of the week
  • Lose 3 pounds this week
  • Go from your couch to running a mile
  • 7 super healthy recipes for each day of the week

Step 2: Create great content that will help others learn
  • Record a YouTube video and load them up for each day in our easy to use builder

  • Finish up with a homework assignment for each day

Step 3: Publish it, price it, promote it!
  • Once you have a great week long course, publish and review it
  • Decide what your knowledge is worth (and change your price anytime)
  • Promote your GroupMind course to your list, friends, & followers!
